Our Story

We Believe Every Pet Deserves Love

PawFinds was founded with one simple goal: to connect animals in need with families who have love to give. We are a student-led project built as part of a web development curriculum, designed to reflect a real-world adoption platform.

Every year, thousands of animals in India are abandoned or left without homes. We believe that adoption — not buying — is the answer to reducing stray animal populations and giving pets the lives they deserve.

Our platform connects rescue shelters, foster homes, and caring individuals to make the adoption process simple, transparent, and heartwarming.

Why It Matters

Pet Adoption in India

India has one of the highest stray animal populations in the world. Adoption is not just kind — it's necessary.

According to the Animal Welfare Board of India, there are over 30 million stray dogs across the country. Each year, countless animals are abandoned after being purchased impulsively, contributing to an already overwhelming shelter system.


When you adopt, you're not just saving one life — you free up space in a shelter for another animal in need. You also send a message against unethical breeding and pet shops that profit from animals as commodities.


PawFinds believes education and easy access to adoption resources can change behaviour. Our platform is designed to make adoption the easier, better, and more joyful choice.
