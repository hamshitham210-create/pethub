The Journey

5 Steps to Adopting

From browsing to bringing your pet home — here's what the process looks like.

1
🔍
Browse Pets

Explore our listings. Use filters to find pets by species, age, or gender until one catches your heart.

2
📋
Fill the Form

Complete the adoption application with your details, living situation, and experience with pets.

3
🏠
Home Verification

Our team does a quick home-check (in-person or virtual) to make sure the environment is safe for the pet.

4
🤝
Meet the Pet

Spend time with your chosen pet at a shelter or foster home. See if you two are a perfect match!

5
🎉
Welcome Home!

Sign the adoption agreement and bring your new family member home. The journey begins!
